What it is we do

Company Overview

SHIELD leverages Design Thinking and digital transformation to empower the youth, businesses and community organizations to acquire the skills, knowledge and competence required to either pursue entrepreneurship or earn employment.

Our goal is to create design thinkers and entrepreneurs who can collaborate to solve social problems experienced in the slums and rural areas and in the process inspire even more fellow youths to join the movement thus bringing about a ripple effect of positive change in their communities.

The SHIELD Solution

  • Relevant Skills
  • Quality Education
  • Impact

Relevant Skills

We are an impact organization whose goal is to drive transformative change for youth in Africa by powering them with relevant Entrepreneurial and Tech skills through the provision of quality and decentralized training.

Quality Education

We aim to empower African youth with skills that allow you to navigate the job market with confidence. In addition to empowering them with the knowledge to create jobs that sustain themselves through entrepreneurship.

Impact

Africa faces a huge digital skills gap that dilutes economic opportunities and development caused largely in part by lack of access and in-affordability. We offer free and accessible design thinking and tech education to bridge this gap.

Will I have to pay to be trained in Design Thinking?

No, the course is absolutely free ⚡

How do I access my course?

You can access your course by signing in through SHIELD'S learning management platform here. You will log in with the email and password created at the time of registration.

How many hours on average per day do I need to successfully undertake the course?

You need at least 3 hours a day, on average 18 hrs a week

What is the procedure of undertaking the courses?

You choose your own procedure because It is self paced paced learning experience.

Within SHIELD, you would learn design thinking, software development, as well as be placed into a software development program as well as be placed a pre-accelerator.
