Company Overview

Avacus mission is to connect people is what makes this statement so strong. And that promise has gone beyond their website and walls of their food stops: The sweetgreen team has made strides in the communities where they've opened stores as well. Primarily, they provide education to young kids on healthy eating, fitness, sustainability, and where food comes from.

Our sweetlife music festival attracts 20,000 like-minded people every year who come together to listen to music, eat healthy food, and give back to a cause -- their sweetgreen in schools charity partner, FoodCorps. If your company has a similar focus on growing your business and giving back.
